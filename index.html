Hi! I'm a US Army veteran trained in using, maintaining and training users of Army communications equipment. I have 20 years experience making people's lives easier as an Executive Assistant. In 2017, I changed directions. I moved into IT as an associate programmer and co-managed an Enterprise level Skype for Business hybrid (Unified Communications) platform for 19,000+ users. In 2018, my company was purchased and in early 2019, I was laid off. I'm a graduate of Eleven Fifty Academy's 12 week Software Development course and taken two of their intro to coding for HTML & CSS courses and am excited to learn more!
I am currently a student in Eleven Fifty Academy's JavaScript Web Development Program.

I love creating things that help people in real world settings. I want to be a problem-solver, a self-starter, a helper, and a creator. Full-stack development allows me to be better at those things.
